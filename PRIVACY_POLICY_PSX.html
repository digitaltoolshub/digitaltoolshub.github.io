Privacy Policy for Pakistan Stock Advisor

Last Updated: July 11, 2026  |  Effective Date: July 9, 2026

Introduction

Digital Tools Hub ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Pakistan Stock Advisor mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when using the App. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices while delivering powerful stock analysis tools for the Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX).

Information We Collect

1. Optional Account Information

You can use most of the App without creating an account. If you choose to sign in, we collect:

  • Email address — when you register with email/password or sign in with Google.
  • Authentication provider — whether you used Google Sign-In or email/password.
  • Firebase User ID (UID) — a unique anonymous identifier generated by Firebase Authentication.

This information is used to authenticate you, sync app data across devices, and manage your premium subscription status.

2. Device & App Metadata

When you use the App, we may collect limited non-personal technical information:

  • Mobile operating system and OS version (Android/iOS)
  • App version number
  • Device timezone and locale/language settings
  • Anonymous Firebase Analytics identifiers

This data helps us understand app performance, usage trends, and improve reliability. Some of this information may be shared with Google Analytics through Firebase Analytics user properties so we can measure usage at an aggregate level.

3. App Usage & Aggregate Metrics

With your consent and where permitted by your device settings, we collect anonymous, aggregated metrics such as:

  • Number of portfolios, transactions, alerts, and watchlists created
  • Whether CSV import has been used
  • Feature usage patterns (e.g., screening, chart analysis)

Analytics events and user properties are designed not to include stock symbols, transaction quantities or prices, portfolio identifiers, alert values, or other details of individual investment decisions. These measurements are used only for aggregate analytics and product improvement.

4. Advertising Identifier

For users on the free tier, the App uses Google Mobile Ads. Google's advertising services may access your device's Advertising ID to deliver relevant ads. Premium subscribers do not see ads and this identifier is not used for advertising purposes.

You can reset or limit your Advertising ID through your device settings at any time.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Bank account, brokerage, or payment card details (purchases are handled by Apple/Google/RevenueCat)
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files beyond CSV files you explicitly choose to import

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core App functionality (stock screening, charts, portfolios, alerts)
  • Authenticate users and enable cloud sync of watchlists, portfolios, transactions, alerts, and preferences
  • Process and restore premium subscriptions
  • Show advertisements to free-tier users
  • Analyze anonymous usage trends to improve the App
  • Maintain App security and prevent abuse
  • Respond to user support requests

Local Data Storage

Much of your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Stock screening results and filter preferences
  • Portfolios, transactions, and dividend records
  • Price alerts and watchlist settings
  • Theme preferences and chart settings
  • Cached market data for offline access

Local data can be cleared by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's storage from your device settings.

Cloud Data & Sync

If you sign in, your custom watchlists/indexes, portfolios and their transactions, price alerts, preferences, and limited account metadata are stored in Firebase Firestore to enable cross-device synchronization. Portfolio transaction records can include stock symbols, quantities, prices, dates, and optional notes. Alert records can include stock symbols, target values, and condition types. This cloud data is associated with your authenticated Firebase account.

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate the App:

  • Firebase Authentication — User sign-in (email/Google) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Firestore — Cloud sync of watchlists, portfolios, transactions, alerts, and preferences — Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics — Anonymous usage analytics — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — Displaying ads to free users — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Social authentication — Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management & purchases — RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • PSX Market Data Sources — Public stock prices and financial data

These providers may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Advertising

  • The free version of the App displays interstitial and app-open advertisements through Google Mobile Ads.
  • Ads may be personalized using your device's Advertising ID where permitted by your settings.
  • Pakistan Stock Advisor Pro subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

Premium Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed through RevenueCat and platform app stores (Google Play / Apple App Store). We do not handle or store your payment information. RevenueCat provides us with subscription status so we can unlock premium features and remove ads.

Push Notifications

If you enable price alerts, the App will request permission to send push notifications. Notifications are used only to notify you when your configured alert conditions are met. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Internet Access — to fetch PSX market data and enable cloud features
  • Notifications — to deliver price alerts
  • Advertising ID — to support personalized advertising for free users
  • Storage / File Access — only when you choose to import or export portfolio CSV files

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Authentication is handled by Firebase, which uses industry-standard security practices.
  • Cloud-synced data is stored in secure Firebase infrastructure.
  • Local data is protected by your device's operating system security.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local data is retained on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its storage.
  • Cloud-synced account data, including watchlists, portfolios, transactions, alerts, and preferences, is retained while your account is active. If you delete your account, associated cloud data is removed.
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained by Firebase according to Google's data retention policies.

To request deletion of your account and associated cloud data, contact us at the email below or use the account deletion option in the App settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

International Users

The App can be used globally, but it focuses on the Pakistan Stock Exchange. Your data may be processed by third-party services (such as Firebase and RevenueCat) in accordance with their privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Post the revised policy in the App and its documentation

Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
